BMW M8

History

In the early 1990s BMW was working on a prototype car based on their E36 8 coup√©, an M8 prototype. The M8 prototype sported a 6.0 litre V12 engine that was capable of 300 kmph and 640 horsepower. In comparison at that time, BMWs 850Csi which produced only 380 horsepower. However despite these incredible numbers, the car remained under wraps until 2010 where it wasfinally unveiled. After that the first official M8 was released in 2019 and caused a sensation for the M series.


Engine

The 2021 BMW M8 comes equipped with an M TwinPower Turbo 8 cylinder 6.0 litre V8 engine that can reach speeds of up to 305 kmph with a fast 0-100 time of only 3.3 seconds. The engine delivers 617 horsepower with an 8-speed Septonic transmission and outstanding cross cylinder bank exhaust manifold for optimum response characteristics.


Luxury

The BMW M8 comes equipped with carbon fiber grille, mirror caps and side gills for a strong sporty look. The seats are leather trim "Merino" alcalantara with side bolsters for comfort and a sporty feel, a sleek carbonfiber center console with their comfortable grip M gear selector and the M Specific Instrument Cluster heads-up display with full driving information including g-force of longitudinal and laderal acceleration.
